About UBhoko

Rev. Thulasizwe Buthelezi

UBhoko lukaZulu serves as a guiding force for the Zulu Nation. The term "uBhoko" symbolizes resilience and leadership, much like the walking stick that supports and guides. Our mission is deeply rooted in the cultural and historical essence of the AmaZulu people.

At the heart of uBhoko stands Rev. Thulasizwe Buthelezi, uNdunankulu (Prime Minister) of the Zulu King and Nation. With his deep understanding of Zulu heritage and forward-thinking leadership, Rev. Buthelezi plays a vital role in bridging the traditions of the past with the aspirations of the present. His steadfast commitment ensures that uBhoko remains a symbol of unity, progress, and cultural preservation.

Meet the Advisory Board Chairperson

Professor Gugu Mazibuko

Professor Gugu Mazibuko is a distinguished scholar and cultural advocate who serves as the Chairperson of the Advisory Board of uBhoko lukaZulu. A professor at the University of Johannesburg, she is deeply knowledgeable in the traditions, history, and leadership structures of the AmaZulu people.

With her extensive experience and expertise in Zulu culture, Professor Mazibuko plays a pivotal role in guiding UBhoko’s mission to preserve the heritage of the AmaZulu nation while driving innovation and progress. Her leadership ensures that the Advisory Board provides valuable insights and strategic direction for the organization’s initiatives.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To provide guidance, insights, innovation, and sustainability to the Traditional Prime Minister of AmaZulu.

Our Mission

To advise and support the Traditional Prime Minister through engaged and informed advocacy, fostering development within the Zulu Nation.

Our Values

Providing Strategic Advice

Offering insights to the Traditional Prime Minister of the Zulu Nation.

Sharing Expert Knowledge

Providing specialized knowledge in the development of the Zulu Nation.

Facilitating Discussions

Encouraging brainstorming sessions to develop fresh ideas.

Innovation

Striving for innovative approaches to achieve high-level outcomes.

Leveraging Networks

Connecting stakeholders to achieve mutual goals for the Zulu Nation.

Respect

Treating everyone with respect and dignity.

Integrity

Demonstrating ethical principles in all endeavors.

Excellence

Pursuing the consistent achievement of higher standards.
